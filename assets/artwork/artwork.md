# Artwork

The following are some of my own artwork. They are mixture of draw digital images and physical media.<br><br>

## Dragons
I have always had an interest in dragons. I was affected by the song "Puff the Magic Dragon" as a child. I also read a lot of fantasy literature. Dragons show up frequently in my work. I taught myself how to draw expressive lines and shapes with dragon faces and heads.

## Various Monsters
One of the secrets of drawing monsters is you cannot draw them wrong. All the mistakes add up to something more monstrous, so when you make one you can just add to it. I enjoy the idea of monsters not so different from ourselves, with similar interests, fascinations and problems.

## Spookiness
My favorite attraction at Disneyland is the Haunted Mansion. As a kid I used to have dreams the ride was much more elabortate, much longer, and quite a bit more frightening. One of the things I like on the ride are the portraits in the hallway. Not the ones that change shape, but the tiny little drawings of various ghosts and ghasts and ghouls. I wanted to capture that feel in the some of the following images.

The second image is of a performance with Brittany Haas (fiddle), Chris Thile (mandolin, or course), Bela Fleck (banjo - what else?), Chris Edridge (guitar), and the name of the bass player escapes me.<br>
